Each month, I spend around 900 EUR. Out of that amount:

  • 61.1%
    goes towards basic living costs. rent, bills, Internet connection, food
  • 20.0%
    goes towards luxury. caffeine, niccotine, alcohol
  • 10.2%
    goes towards my education. MOOCs, books, certifications
  • 5.4%
    goes towards infrastructure. servers, domains, subscriptions
  • 3.3%
    gets donated to a non-profit. EFF, Tor Project, Internet Archive, Wikimedia Foundation
